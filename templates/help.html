📌 Frequently Asked Questions

🔐 I forgot my PIN. What should I do?

Go to the Forget PIN page, enter your account number and security answer. A new PIN will be sent to your registered email.

💳 Why is my account locked?

Your account gets locked after 3 incorrect PIN attempts. It will automatically unlock after 3 minutes.

💸 Why can't I withdraw or transfer money?

You might have exceeded your daily limit. You can update limits in the Change Limit section.

📧 I didn’t receive email notifications

Check spam folder. Sometimes SMTP delays may occur due to server issues.

📘 How to Use NexVault

  • 🆕 Register your account with name, email, and PIN
  • 🔐 Login using Account Number + PIN
  • 💰 Deposit / Withdraw money
  • 🔁 Transfer funds to another account
  • 📊 View transaction history and statements
  • ⚙️ Update limits and change PIN

🛡️ Security Tips

  • Never share your PIN with anyone
  • Always logout after using your account
  • Use strong and unique PIN
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
